Core governance constituency with full voting authority — those who have demonstrated through sustained action that they can be trusted with collective decision-making.

## Purpose

Members are the citizens of this network nation — the backbone of commons governance. Every decision flows through their consent. Crossing this membrane means the community has witnessed your alignment and formally recognized it.

## Criteria

- [[Participant]] with sustained aligned participation
- Nominated by a current [[Member]]
- Approved through 3-member consent + 48h objection window
- No unresolved paramount objections
- Ongoing commitment to governance participation

## Responsibilities

**Governance:** Participate in consent processes, counted in quorum, propose and consent

**Nomination:** Nominate aligned Participants for membership

**Treasury Oversight:** View all treasury transactions, participate in allocation decisions

**Accountability:** Willingness to hold and be held accountable

**Domain Contribution:** Active contribution to at least one area of commons work

## Benefits

| Category | Access |
|----------|--------|
| **Governance** | Full voting rights, counted in quorum, propose and consent |
| **Channels** | All channels including `#stewardship` (read access) |
| **Treasury** | Viewer access — see all transactions, balances, allocation history |
| **Knowledge Commons** | Full admin access, create structures |

**Note:** As a Member, you are also a [[Participant]] and retain all Participant rights.

## Membrane Crossing

To become a [[Steward]]:
1. Any Member may be nominated (including self-nomination)
2. Full commons consent process: all Members, 72h objection window
3. No unresolved paramount objections
4. Monthly rotation ensures no accumulation of positional power
