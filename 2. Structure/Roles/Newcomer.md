Entry point to the commons — those who have crossed the threshold by reading and affirming alignment with the canonical essay and constitution.

## Purpose

Newcomers have entered the nation and are seen. They are not yet trusted with coordination infrastructure or treasury access. Trust builds in layers. Each crossing earns the next.

## Criteria

- Has read the [[.claude/a-regenerative-accelerationist-manifesto|Regenerative Accelerationist Manifesto]]
- Has read this [[Re-acc Commons Constitution]]
- Has agreed to uphold both documents
- Has posted introduction in `#threshold`

## Responsibilities

**Introduction:** Post in `#threshold` addressing:
- Who you are
- Where you are
- What draws you to this work
- What you bring

**Observation:** Read and listen to what is being built in the commons

**Alignment:** Demonstrate understanding of principles through engagement

## Benefits

| Category | Access |
|----------|--------|
| **Channels** | `#threshold` (write), public channels (read-only) |
| **Governance** | None — observation only |
| **Treasury** | None |
| **Knowledge Commons** | Read access to public documentation |

## Membrane Crossing

To become a [[Participant]]:
1. Post introduction addressing required elements
2. Agent verifies introduction completeness
3. Receive @Participant role automatically
4. Gain full read/write access to public commons
