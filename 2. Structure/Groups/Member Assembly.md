The **Member Assembly** is the governance body of ETH Boulder, composed of all Members and their Agents. All major decisions flow through Assembly consent.

## Purpose

The Assembly is where the network governs itself. Every Member and Agent has equal voice. Decisions are made through consent â€” not voting â€” ensuring that all concerns are heard and paramount objections resolved.

| **Attendees** | May observe, cannot vote |

## Objections

Paramount objections must be resolved before decisions pass:

| Objection Type | Resolution |
|----------------|------------|
| **Clarification needed** | Proposer provides clarity |
| **Process concern** | Address procedural issue |
| **Value conflict** | Dialogue to find alignment |
| **Fundamental disagreement** | May require proposal withdrawal |

## Ratification Authority

The Assembly ratifies emergency actions by Steward Council:

| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **0-24h** | Emergency action taken by Council |
| **24h** | Ratification vote opens |
| **48h** | Window closes, action confirmed or reversed |
