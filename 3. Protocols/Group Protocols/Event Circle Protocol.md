This protocol governs the formation, operation, and coordination of the [[Event Circle]] — the body responsible for stewarding the annual ETH Boulder convening.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Continuous (circle operates year-round)

**Phase-Dependent Intensity:**
- Inter-Event: Maintenance mode
- Pre-Event: Full activation
- Event: Maximum intensity
- Post-Event: Wind-down and retrospective

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| Circle Members | Session coordination, logistics | [[Network Assembly]] |
| Steering Committee | Event-level decisions, budget | [[Network Assembly]] |
| Steward Lead | Real-time event decisions | Steering Committee |
| [[Network Assembly]] | Budget approval, format changes | Constitution |

## Steering Committee

### Composition

- 3-5 Members selected annually
- At least one Steward serves on committee
- Selection through network consent (3-member + 48h)

### Selection Timeline

| Step | Timing | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Post-Event (March) | Call for nominations |
| 2 | Post-Event (March) | Self-nomination or nomination |
| 3 | Post-Event (March) | Consent process |
| 4 | Post-Event (April) | Committee seated for following year |

### Committee Responsibilities

- Budget development and stewardship
- Venue relationship management
- Speaker outreach
- Format decisions within approved parameters
- Retrospective facilitation

## Event Cycle Operations

### Inter-Event Phase (Mar-Nov)

| Activity | Responsibility |
|----------|----------------|
| Retrospective integration | Steering Committee |
| Venue relationship maintenance | Steering + Third Space Circle |
| Early logistics planning | Steering Committee |
| Knowledge graph enrichment | Circle + Knowledge Circle |

### Pre-Event Phase (Dec-Jan)

| Week | Activities |
|------|-----------|
| Dec 1-15 | Budget approval, venue confirmation, speaker outreach |
| Dec 15-Jan 1 | Session proposals open, volunteer recruitment |
| Jan 1-15 | Session proposals continue, logistics coordination |
| Jan 15-25 | QV voting period, schedule draft |
| Jan 25-31 | Schedule finalization, volunteer assignments |

### Event Phase (Feb - 3 days)

| Responsibility | Lead |
|----------------|------|
| Real-time coordination | Steward Lead |
| Venue liaison | Third Space Circle liaison |
| Session facilitation support | Volunteers |
| Documentation | Knowledge Circle liaison |
| Emergency decisions | Steward Lead |

### Post-Event Phase (March)

| Activity | Timeline |
|----------|----------|
| Vendor payments | Week 1 |
| Retrospective facilitation | Week 2 |
| Knowledge graph enrichment | Week 2-4 |
| Fast-track membership nominations | Week 2-4 |
| Committee succession | Week 4 |

## Decision Authority

| Decision Type | Authority | Process |
|--------------|-----------|---------|
| Session scheduling | Circle + QV results | Per [[3. Protocols/Asset Protocols/Session Proposal Protocol|Session Proposal Protocol]] |
| Budget allocation | [[Network Assembly]] | Standard consent |
| Venue selection | Joint with Third Space | Circle consent |
| Format changes | [[Network Assembly]] | Standard consent |
| Real-time event decisions | Steward Lead | Immediate, logged |
| Speaker invitations | Steering Committee | Committee consent |

### Budget Process

| Step | Action | Actor | Timeline |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Draft budget | Steering Committee | Nov |
| 2 | Submit for consent | Steering Committee | Early Dec |
| 3 | Consent process | Network Assembly | Dec |
| 4 | Budget approved | Network Assembly | Before Pre-Event |
| 5 | Spending per budget | Circle | Per approval |
| 6 | Variance reporting | Steering Committee | Monthly |

### Emergency Event Decisions

During the event, Steward Lead has authority to:
- Adjust schedule for emergencies
- Handle safety issues
- Make real-time logistics decisions
- Spend within emergency reserve

All emergency decisions documented and reviewed in retrospective.

## Coordination with Other Circles

| Circle | Coordination |
|--------|--------------|
| [[Knowledge Circle]] | Session documentation, graph enrichment |
| [[Third Space Circle]] | Venue partnerships, physical logistics |
| [[Grants Circle]] | Hackathon prizes, speaker grants |

### Joint Meetings

Pre-Event phase: Weekly coordination meetings with all circles

## Agent Participation

[[Agent|Agents]] support the Event Circle through:
- QV aggregation and schedule generation
- Logistics tracking and conflict detection
- Real-time event coordination
- Documentation and knowledge graph updates
