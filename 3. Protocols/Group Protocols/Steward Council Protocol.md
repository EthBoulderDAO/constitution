This protocol governs how the Steward Council operates — elections, meetings, oversight, emergencies, and disputes.

## Composition

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Size** | 5-7 members |
| **Eligibility** | Any Member in good standing |
| **Term** | 12 months |
| **Staggered** | Not all seats open simultaneously |

---

## Elections

### When Seats Open

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Term expiration** | Seat opens for election |
| **Resignation** | Seat opens immediately |
| **Recall** | Seat opens upon successful recall |

### Election Process

| Step | Duration | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | 1 week | Nomination period (self or other) |
| 2 | — | Nominees confirm acceptance |
| 3 | 48h | Member Assembly consent |
| 4 | — | Results certified by current Council |
| 5 | 2 weeks | Transition period |

### Multiple Candidates

If multiple candidates for one seat:
- Run separate consent processes
- First to achieve consent wins
- Or: ranked consent (Assembly ranks preferences)

---

## Meetings

| Type | Frequency | Quorum |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Regular** | Monthly minimum | Majority |
| **Emergency** | As needed | Majority |
| **Async** | Ongoing | N/A |

### Meeting Format

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Function Lead reports** | Review monthly reports |
| **Open issues** | Discuss ongoing matters |
| **Decisions** | Consent on council matters |
| **Next steps** | Assign actions |

---

## Oversight

### Function Lead Oversight

| Lead | Council Reviews |
|------|-----------------|
| **Event Lead** | Event cycle progress, budget execution |
| **Treasury Lead** | Financial reporting, compliance |
| **Knowledge Lead** | Graph health, moderation actions |
| **Third Space Lead** | Partnership status |

### Oversight Actions

| Situation | Council Action |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Missed report** | Follow up with lead |
| **Performance concern** | Private conversation, support |
| **Persistent issues** | Request lead step down |
| **Removal** | Require Assembly ratification |

---

## Emergency Authority

### Scope

Council can take emergency action for:
- Immediate threats to network safety
- Urgent operational needs
- Time-sensitive external requirements

### Process

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Immediate | Council takes action (majority) |
| 2 | Immediately | Document action and rationale |
| 3 | 24h | Open ratification window |
| 4 | 48h | Assembly ratifies or reverses |

### Constraints

Council emergency authority does NOT include:
- Constitutional amendments
- Permanent membership changes
- Budget increases beyond approved

---

## Dispute Resolution

### Process

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Receive dispute from participant(s) |
| 2 | Assign mediator (Steward without conflict) |
| 3 | Facilitate dialogue |
| 4 | Work toward resolution |
| 5 | If unresolved, escalate to Assembly |

### Recusal

Stewards must recuse from disputes involving:
- Themselves
- Close collaborators
- Conflicts of interest

---

## Council Decisions

### Decision Process

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Matter raised |
| 2 | Discussion |
| 3 | Proposal formed |
| 4 | Consent (majority for operational, all for significant) |

### Documentation

All Council decisions:
- Recorded in council channel
- Logged in Knowledge Graph
- Reported to Assembly (monthly summary)

---

## Accountability

### To Member Assembly

| Mechanism | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Monthly report** | Summary of Council activities |
| **Ratification** | Emergency actions require ratification |
| **Recall** | Assembly can recall Stewards |

### Recall Process

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Member proposes recall |
| 2 | 72h consent window |
| 3 | All member participation required |
| 4 | If passed, Steward removed |

## Transition

### Outgoing Steward

| Task | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| **Document state** | Before departure |
| **Brief successor** | 2-week transition |
| **Transfer access** | At transition |

### Incoming Steward

| Task | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| **Receive briefing** | 2-week transition |
| **Shadow meetings** | During transition |
| **Full participation** | After transition |
