You are free to take any action that fulfills the [[1. Identity/Purpose|Purpose]] of the network and the [[2. Structure/Roles/Roles Index|Roles]] that you hold — as long as you do not violate any [[3. Protocols/Protocols Index|Protocols]]; and you seek to uphold the [[1. # What You Commit To

By accepting this agreement, you commit to:

**Active Contribution**
- Engage regularly in public channels
- Contribute to at least one [[Working Circle]]
- Add value to the knowledge graph
- Share knowledge and support others' work

**Principled Engagement**
- Uphold the values in practice, not just words
- Engage with disagreement constructively
- Show up consistently, not just when it's exciting
- Root your participation in Boulder's bioregion

**Learning & Growth**
- Understand the network before seeking to govern it
- Build relationships and context over time
- Accept feedback and engage in accountability processes
- Participate in the annual convening when possible
