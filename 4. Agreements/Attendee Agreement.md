This agreement defines the commitments of all ETH Boulder Attendees.

## Who This Applies To

Anyone who has attended an ETH Boulder event and been verified as an Attendee.

## What You Affirm

By participating as an Attendee, you affirm:

### Alignment with Principles

- I have read and resonate with the [[.claude/ethereum-localism-principles|Ethereum Localism Principles]]
- I understand ETH Boulder's purpose, values, and mission
- I commit to engaging with the network in good faith

### Community Participation

- I will treat all participants with respect
- I will engage constructively in discussions
- I will seek understanding before judgment
- I will raise concerns through appropriate channels

### Knowledge Graph Contribution

- I will contribute authentic, accurate information
- I will respect the work of others in the graph
- I will not vandalize or intentionally corrupt data
- I will help maintain the collective intelligence

### Event Participation

- I will abide by event guidelines and code of conduct
- I will respect venue rules and third space agreements
- I will contribute to the convening experience

## What You Can Expect

As an Attendee, you can expect:

- **Graph access** — Read and write permissions to the Knowledge Graph
- **Community participation** — Access to discussions and activities
- **Event access** — Participation in future ETH Boulder events
- **Membership path** — Eligibility to be nominated for Membership

## Path to Membership

To become a Member:
1. Demonstrate ongoing participation beyond the event
2. Receive nomination from an existing Member
3. Pass the consent process (48h, 3+ member consent)

## Duration

This agreement applies for as long as you participate in ETH Boulder activities.
